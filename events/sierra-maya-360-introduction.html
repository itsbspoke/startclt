The Managing Partners

Amish Shah

Amish has been an entrepreneur since the age of 25, when he started his first company in 1998. He is a visionary and an innovator with a relentless drive to be the best. He has a proven track record in leadership, sales, marketing, strategy and recruiting. Venture Capital firms, C-level executives, and Founders of early and mid-stage startups from around the globe call on him directly for his consultation and advisement before making major decisions that will have an impact on their bottom line. Amish’s passion is contagious to the people around him, and his results oriented approach of getting things done paired with his success over the years gives him the perfect background and keen eye for SierraMaya360 when evaluating potential investments. He is a frequent guest on CNBC, Bloomberg and Fox News.

Eric Kagan

Eric has founded multiple companies with a focus on technology. His technical, operational and finance background is the perfect mix and allows him to understand all aspects of a business. He has experience with various levels of financing from traditional senior bank lending to mezzanine debt and equity, and recently sold his company to a private equity firm. In the past year, Eric has invested in several startup companies and has assisted the founders and management teams to understand what it takes to build and grow a successful company. Eric’s experience along with Amish's background allows SierraMaya360 to approach any potential investment situation with eagerness and confidence that they can provide invaluable resources in addition to just a financial investment.

Glen Howard

With deep involvement in Silicon Valley, Glen brings a solid track record that has been focused on startup and venture capital financing since 1997, with participation in the debt and equity financing of over 250 early stage companies. Glen specializes in startup development and brings an expertise in fundraising. Armed with both an Engineering degree and an MBA, Glen has experienced great success as an entrepreneur, and was instrumental in taking the company he co-founded public. He has been a Managing Director for multiple venture firms, has provided consulting services to startups, and has managed numerous other organizations investing in alternative assets such as real estate, equities and futures. Glen’s well-respected reputation and experience delivers resources, knowledge and guidance to each of SierraMaya360's investments.

Tony Potts

Seen by millions daily spanning three decades on NBC’s hit show Access Hollywood, Tony is instantly recognizable to millions of Americans and has been a fixture in Hollywood since 1997 and Silicon Beach since 2009. Tony is a successful real estate investor, entrepreneur, content creator, startup advisor and consultant. He is a trusted advisor to A-list stars, studios, directors and executives while his global reach extends to the world’s biggest stages: Oscars, Olympics, Grammys, Golden Globes, London, Paris, Cannes,, Monte Carlo, Rome and beyond.
