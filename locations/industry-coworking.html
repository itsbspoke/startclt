Coworking

  • Hosting a creative community

    At Start Charlotte, we love coworking and its conscientious proprietors. Tyler Ford started telling the coworking story with his first location, Lightbulb Coworking on Mint Ave. in the South End. Two moves later, Industry is now moving to the NC Music Factory into 10,000 sq.ft.

    We will have a full profile in time, but for now, most people need to know how to get there for the SierraMaya360 introduction.

Directions

1000 NC Music Factory Blvd, 28206

Google Maps knows where it is. Most of us will head north on Graham St. and take a left onto NC Music Factory Blvd. Take the first parking you come to on the right, in parking lot #1.
As you exit your vehicle, continue walking in the direction you've been driving, past Label towards most of the buildings. Angle towards the fountain after you pass the Mexican restaurant on your right. The door you're looking for is under an OSSO sign at this writing.
Industry is past two sets of double-doors. In general, you need someone to let you in the second double doors, but if we're hosting the event, we'll have that covered for you.
